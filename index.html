About Me

In the far 1987 I started to be computer literate, that’s more than half of my life age and for most of that time I’ve been engaged working on and with computer.

“Kvazar’ computer center which I will always remember - your black-green eye monitors, plotters which could be equivalent to today’s small apartment and their disc drives - which could only be used by heave them together.

But then I fell in love with it! This was a love for life. It was with these i received my higher degree in education and subsequent related jobs. Hundred of projects, thousands of miles crossed, great cities that were visited and with thousands of people met and communicated with.

I like to meet and communicate with people, this gives me better understanding of their needs, their thinking, their experience and to at last understand what they wanted of me which i in returns, I pass back to them ideas and experience.

And yet, every new project is like meeting with new personalities, experiences and persuasions with new ‘map of reality’. comparing and matching of new challenges - that’s the real quest!

For these reasons, I prefer to work on a “turn-key” projects. Where I could see the results of my labour and satisfaction on the face of my customers. That inspires and it helps.

The final results sometimes come out to be very different from the initial goals with a more than an half margin. but I’m absolutely sure that after severe discussions, explanations, and sometimes arguments the customers will at the end get a PROJECT within “ margin of safety”

Sometimes after completion of a new project, I ask myself - if there is anything else that I could have done better?

And that gives a call that the next project must be taken care of better that the previous one.
